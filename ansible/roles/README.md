# Ansible Roles

This directory contains individual, reusable components for managing specific parts of the system.

## Roles

- **benchmark_models**: A role for benchmarking models.
- **bootstrap_agent**: A role for bootstrapping an agent.
- **common**: A role for common configuration.
- **common-tools**: A role for common tools.
- **config_manager**: A role for managing configuration.
- **consul**: A role for managing Consul.
- **desktop_extras**: A role for installing desktop extras.
- **docker**: A role for managing Docker.
- **download_models**: A role for downloading models.
- **home_assistant**: A role for managing Home Assistant.
- **kittentts**: A role for managing KittenTTS.
- **llama_cpp**: A role for managing llama.cpp.
- **mqtt**: A role for managing MQTT.
- **nfs**: A role for managing NFS.
- **nixos_pxe_server**: A role for managing a NixOS PXE server.
- **nomad**: A role for managing Nomad.
- **paddler**: A role for managing the paddler.
- **pipecatapp**: A role for managing the pipecatapp application.
- **power_manager**: A role for managing the power manager.
- **provisioning_api**: A role for managing the provisioning API.
- **pxe_server**: A role for managing a PXE server.
- **python_deps**: A role for managing Python dependencies.
- **system_deps**: A role for managing system dependencies.
- **term_everything**: A role for managing term_everything.
- **vision**: A role for managing vision.
- **whisper_cpp**: A role for managing whisper.cpp.
- **world_model_service**: A role for managing the world model service.
